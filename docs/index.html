Golden Retriever

The service dog of America

Polo ( The Golden Retriever ) had just finish a long water fowl trainning session at the lake. He is now relaxing, and enjoying his great rewards. Which is a plastic duck decoy.

This page is dedicated to the Golden Retriever. They are a great family companion, a best friend, and one of America best services dog. Their original purpose was to serve hunter in the field, where they would help search and retrieve fallen birds. Their charming, intelligent, and loyal personality have found their way from the hunting field to serving humans as services dog. With their many talents and the eagerness to learn and please their master. They can be trained as a guide dog to help the blind and people with disabilities, a narcotic & bomb-sniffing dog, & therapy dog where they visit hospital and nursing home to comfort many patients with warm hug & companionship. Hopefully, we all can understand & appreciate the Golden Retriever & the wonderful services they have provided us.
